🗽 Fair Fees Distribution ✅ Fees will be transferred to Talents, Agents and Mentors if the mission is over and well evaluated.
Otherwise, they will be transferred to the DAO's Wallet. ✅ The incentives mechanism, base of the Tokenomic, improves commitment, involvement and trust. ✅ The distribution sustains an efficient sharing economy.
💎 Escrow Mechanism ✅ Mission payments and protocol fees are funded, protected and locked before delivery. ✅ Clients and Talents can control and perform fast secure crypto-payments.
💰 Staking & Earning ✅ In order to be a fellowship member and take the fees, Mentors and Agents must stake.
They will get additional rewards, considering lock time and amount. ✅ People are “skin-in-the-game” and focused on success and excellence...
This is exactly why the stakes should not be slashed !
In any case, Mentors and Agents will get back their stakes. ✅ Stakers will get a double benefit : distributed fees + staking rewards !
🧙‍♂️ Mentoring Program ✅ Mentors are involved in delivery because they are “skin-in-the-game” !
They provide technical support and assistance to their mentees. ✅ They are very important for the community enablement / excellence.
They train, coach and help their mentees to improve their skills. ✅ Providing learning programs and assets will be promoted and well rewarded ! ✅ This approach also prevents social isolation and help young Talents to become though leader or technical wizards.
